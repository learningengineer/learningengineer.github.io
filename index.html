Find the Subject Level 1
Find the Subject requires that the learner find the subject in a sentence by simply clicking on the word in the sentece. This interface is a lot more intuitive and requires fewer interactions and is less complex. I have made several variations of this that include finding the verb and finding the error.
Book
I wrote this book back when I was teaching composition and found that most writing books were heavy on grammar and usage but nothing about content. It contains a text to speech engine so that students can have the book read to them. The book focuses more on content, such as providing factual support for claims rather than baseless opinion, than it does on grammar and usage.
Ask the Brain
I built this AI application to see if it could be a useful resource for students to test whether or not their sentence is complete or not.
Find the Error: Subject / Verb Agreement
This is a variation of Find the Subject. Here you are finding the Subject / Verb error. Click on the problem in the sentence. The user gets three attempts. After three attempts, the user is told the correct answer and an explanation of the correct answer is provided.

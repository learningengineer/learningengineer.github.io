Titles

The following titles should be italicized.

  1. Books, Movies, TV Shows
  2. Magazines, Newspapers
  3. Paintings, Sculptures, Works of Art
  4. Music Albums

Examples

  1. Books: Of Mice and Men, Outliers, Silent Spring, 1984
  2. Movies and TV Shows: Star Wars, American Sniper, The Walking Dead, Seinfeld
  3. Publications: Newsweek, Sports Illustrated, Janesville Gazette, The New York Times
  4. Painting and Sculptures: The Scream, Guernica, The Thinker, David
  5. Albums: Led Zeppelin IV, Another Level, X, 1989, Ten, 4

Titles

The following titles should be placed in quotation marks.

  1. short stories, essays, articles
  2. songs, poems, skit, short Youtube video
  3. television episodes, book chapters, commercial

Examples

  1. Poems: "Ulysses," "If," "Do Not Go Gentle Into That Goodnight"
  2. Short Stories: "The Lottery," "The Raft," "The Pit and the Pendulum"
  3. Essays: "The Missing Ingredient," "The Checklist," "In Her Defense, I'm Sure the Moose Had It Coming"
  4. Songs: "Over the Rainbow," "Do I Wanna Know," "Jump Around"
  5. TV Episodes and Commercials: "Schweddy Balls," "Soup Nazi," "Just Do It," "Breakfast with Nanna"
  6. Articles: "How trustworthy are electronic voting systems in the US?" "The Internet Has Become the External Hard Drive for Our Memories," "Wisconsin voucher students lag in latest state test"

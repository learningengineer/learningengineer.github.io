Universal Design

What is wrong with universal design?

What is Universal Design in Education?

  1. Analogy that comes from architecture.
    1. Example: A ramp can be used by everyone and not just someone in a wheelchair.
  2. Professes that what is good for disabled students is good for everyone.
    1. Example: Cognitive scaffolding is good for every student and should not be removed.
    2. "The student isn't disabled, the curriculum is."

What is wrong with Universal Design?

  1. It is an analogy from architecture. Learning isn't architecture.
  2. Inferred from Neuroscience--no controlled experiments.
  3. Cognitive scaffolding isn't supposed to be permanent unless student is permanently disabled.
  4. Multiple modalities (learning styles) aren a myth and not "real world."
  5. Ignores the split-attention effect.
  6. Scaffolds replace actual knowledge.
